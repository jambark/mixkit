Tequila
Type: Liquor
ABV: 38-40% (76-80 Proof)

Tequila is a distilled spirit made primarily from the blue agave plant, primarily produced in the region surrounding the city of Tequila in Mexico.
It undergoes a meticulous process where the heart of the agave plant, called the piña, is harvested, cooked, fermented, and distilled to produce a wide range of tequila varieties. These include blanco (unaged), reposado (aged 2-12 months), añejo (aged 1-3 years), and extra añejo (aged over 3 years), each offering distinct flavors and characteristics.
Tequila is known for its earthy, herbaceous, and sometimes spicy flavors, with variations in taste influenced by factors such as production methods, aging duration, and agave cultivation techniques. It holds a prominent place in Mexican culture and is celebrated globally in cocktails like the Margarita, as well as being enjoyed neat or on the rocks.
Tequila is used in:
  • The Margarita
  • The Paloma
  • The Tequila Sunrise
  • The Tommy's Margarita
  • The Long Island Iced Tea
100% Agave Tequila
