The Mojito

Ingredients:

45 ml or 1.5 ounces White Rum

20 ml or 4 teaspoons Lime Juice

2 teaspoons White Cane Sugar (or 20 ml or 4 teaspoons Simple Syrup)

6 Mint Sprigs

Soda Water, as needed

Preparation:

Mix Mint Sprigs with Sugar and Lime Juice directly in glass. Add splash of Soda Water and fill with ice. Pour Rum and top with Soda Water. Stir and garnish.

Serve:

Collins Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Mint Sprigs, Lime Slice.
