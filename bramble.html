The Bramble

Ingredients:

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons Gin

25 ml or 5 teaspoons Lemon Juice

15 ml or .5 ounces Crème de Mûre

12.5 ml or 2.5 teaspoons Simple Syrup

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients except Crème de Mûre into cocktail shaker with ice. Shake and strain. Pour Crème de Mûre into glass in circular motion.

Serve:

Old Fashioned Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Blackberries, Lemon Slice.
