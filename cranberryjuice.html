Cranberry Juice
Type: Mixer
Juice

Cranberry juice, crafted from the juice of cranberries, offers a tangy and refreshing flavor profile characterized by its bright red hue and a balance of sweetness and tartness.
In mixology, cranberry juice serves as a versatile ingredient that adds both flavor and color to a variety of cocktails. Its tartness can complement a wide range of spirits and mixers, providing a refreshing acidity that enhances the complexity of drinks without overwhelming other flavors.
Bartenders often use cranberry juice to create balanced and vibrant cocktails, leveraging its natural tartness to create refreshing mixed drinks suitable for a range of palates and occasions.
