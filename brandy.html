Brandy
Type: Liquor
ABV: 35-60% (70-120 Proof)

Brandy is a distilled spirit made from fermented fruit juice, most commonly derived from grapes but also from other fruits like apples, pears, and cherries. It undergoes a double distillation process to concentrate its flavors and alcohol content.
Some styles of Brandy are aged in oak barrels to develop rich flavors of fruit, spice, and vanilla. Brandy is revered for its complexity and depth, often enjoyed neat or as a key ingredient in classic cocktails such as the Sidecar or Alexander.
Its centuries-old tradition and craftsmanship make brandy a symbol of refinement and sophistication in the world of spirits.
Brandy is used in:
  • The Angel Face
  • The Brandy Crusta
  • The Horse's Neck
  • The Paradise
  • The Porto Flip
  • The Suffering Bastard
  • The Ve.n.to
Apricot Brandy
In The Paradise, the Brandy which is used is specified as Apricot Brandy. It is also used in addition to Calvados in The Angel Face.
Calvados
Calvados is a type of Brandy used in addition to Apricot Brandy in The Angel Face.
Grappa
In The Ve.n.to, the Brandy which is used is specified as Grappa.
