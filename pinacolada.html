The Piña Colada

Ingredients:

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons White Rum

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons Pineapple Juice

30 ml or 1 ounce Coconut Cream

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients with ice into blender. Blend, pour into glass, and garnish.

Serve:

Poco Grande Glass.

Garnishes:

Pineapple Slice, Maraschino Cherry.
