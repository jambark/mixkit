The Casino

Ingredients:

40 ml or 1 ounces and 2 teaspoons Gin

10 ml or 2 teaspoons Maraschino Liqueur

10 ml or 2 teaspoons Lemon Juice

2 dashes Orange Bitters

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker with ice cubes. Shake and strain. Garnish and serve without a straw.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Lemon Twist, Maraschino Cherry.
