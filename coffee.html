Coffee
Type: Mixer
Beverage

Coffee, derived from roasted and ground coffee beans, is a beloved beverage enjoyed worldwide for its rich flavor and stimulating effects. In mixology, coffee is utilized in various forms to impart its bold and complex taste to cocktails. It can be brewed and chilled for iced coffee-based drinks or used as a concentrated espresso shot for a stronger flavor profile.
Coffee's deep, bitter-sweet notes add depth and complexity to cocktails, balancing out sweeter or creamier ingredients. Its versatility allows bartenders to incorporate it into a wide range of cocktails, from classic coffee cocktails like Espresso Martinis to innovative creations where coffee serves as a base or accent flavor. Beyond its taste, coffee also contributes a stimulating element, making it a favorite for both its flavor and its energizing qualities in mixology.
