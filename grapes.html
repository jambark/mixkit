Grapes
Type: Garnish
Fruit

Grapes are a versatile fruit in mixology, prized for their juicy sweetness and ability to lend both flavor and texture to drinks. Whether used fresh or in the form of juices, purees, or extracts, grapes offer a natural sweetness that balances well with a variety of spirits and other ingredients.
Their flavor profile can range from tart and crisp in green grapes to rich and fruity in red or black varieties, allowing mixologists to create a wide spectrum of flavors and aromas in cocktails. Grapes are often muddled or blended to release their juices and enhance the overall depth of a drink, adding a refreshing and vibrant element that complements both classic and innovative cocktail recipes.
Grapes are valued for their versatility in enhancing the visual appeal and taste experience of mixed drinks, making them a popular choice among bartenders seeking to incorporate fresh and natural ingredients into their creations.
