Tabasco Sauce
Type: Mixer
Sauce

Tabasco sauce, a renowned condiment originating from Avery Island, Louisiana, is celebrated for its fiery flavor and versatility in culinary applications. Made from tabasco peppers (Capsicum frutescens var. tabasco), vinegar, and salt, this iconic sauce undergoes a meticulous aging process in oak barrels, which enhances its complex, tangy profile.
The peppers used impart a distinct heat that is both sharp and pungent, balanced by the vinegar's acidity and the salt's savory undertones. Tabasco sauce is prized for its ability to add a spicy kick to a wide range of dishes, from eggs and soups to marinades and cocktails.
Its intense flavor and consistency make it a favorite among chefs and home cooks alike, offering a zesty accent that elevates the taste of food and drinks with just a few drops.
