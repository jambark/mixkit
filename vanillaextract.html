Vanilla Extract
Type: Mixer
Aromatic

Vanilla extract is a concentrated flavoring made from vanilla beans soaked in a mixture of alcohol and water. It captures the rich, sweet aroma and complex flavor profile of vanilla, which includes creamy, floral, and slightly woody notes.
Widely used in baking, desserts, and beverages, vanilla extract enhances dishes with its distinctively warm and aromatic essence. It's a popular choice for adding depth to cakes, cookies, ice creams, and cocktails, prized for its ability to elevate flavors with a touch of natural sweetness and sophistication.
