Tomato Juice
Type: Mixer
Juice

Tomato juice is a savory beverage made from ripe tomatoes that have been crushed, strained, and sometimes blended to achieve a smooth consistency. Known for its vibrant red color and refreshing taste, tomato juice is prized for its rich umami flavor, which comes from the natural glutamates in tomatoes.
It is often enjoyed as a standalone drink, chilled and garnished with celery, salt, and pepper, or used as a base in cocktails like Bloody Marys. Tomato juice is also valued for its nutritional content, being a good source of vitamins C and K, as well as antioxidants like lycopene, which is associated with various health benefits.
Its versatility in both culinary and beverage applications makes tomato juice a popular choice for those seeking a flavorful and nutritious refreshment.
