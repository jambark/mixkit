Honey Syrup
Type: Mixer
Sweetener

Honey syrup, often referred to simply as honey, is a mixture of honey and water, creating a more fluid and mixable consistency compared to straight honey. This preparation ensures that the honey blends smoothly into cocktails without clumping or settling, making it easier to incorporate into drinks.
Honey syrup retains the natural sweetness and flavor of honey while diluting it slightly with water, which helps it integrate seamlessly with other cocktail ingredients.
Bartenders use honey syrup as a versatile sweetening agent in mixology, where it adds a subtle floral sweetness and enhances the overall flavor profile of cocktails without overwhelming the drink. Its consistency and ease of incorporation make honey syrup a preferred choice for creating balanced and flavorful mixed drinks.
Honey Syrup is used in:
  • The Bee's Knees
  • The Penicillin
  • The Spicy Fifty
