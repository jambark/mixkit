The Pisco Sour

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces Pisco

30 ml or 1 ounce Lemon Juice

20 ml or 4 teaspoons Simple Syrup

1 Egg White

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker with ice. Shake, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Goblet, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Few dashes Amargo Bitters.
