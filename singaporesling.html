The Singapore Sling

Ingredients:

120 ml or 4 ounces Pineapple Juice

30 ml or 1 ounce Gin

15 ml or .5 ounces Cherry Liqueur

15 ml or .5 ounces Lime Juice

10 ml or 2 teaspoons Grenadine Syrup

7.5 ml or .5 tablespoons Cointreau

7.5 ml or .5 tablespoons Bénédictine

1 dash Angostura Bitters

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker with ice. Shake, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Hurricane Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Pineapple Slice, Maraschino Cherry.
