Port
Type: Liquor
ABV: 19-22% (38-44 Proof)

Port is a fortified wine with a rich history originating from Portugal's Douro Valley, where it is primarily produced. It distinguishes itself from other grape-based spirits through a unique fortification process: grape spirit is added during fermentation to halt the process early, preserving residual sugars and enhancing alcohol content. This results in a sweet and robust wine known for its deep flavors of dark fruits, nuts, and spices.
Ports are aged in various styles such as Ruby, Tawny, Vintage, and Late Bottled Vintage (LBV), each offering distinct characteristics shaped by maturation in wooden barrels. The aging process contributes to Port's complexity, making it a versatile companion to desserts and cheeses, and a revered component of both Portuguese and international wine cultures.
