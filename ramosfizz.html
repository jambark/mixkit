The Ramos Fizz

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces Cream

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Gin

30 ml or 1 ounce Simple Syrup

15 ml or .5 ounces Lime Juice

15 ml or .5 ounces Lemon Juice

1 Egg White

3 dashes Orange Flower Water

2 drops Vanilla Extract

Soda Water, as needed

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients except soda water into a mixing glass dry (without ice). Shake for two minutes, add ice, shake for one minute. Strain and top with soda water.

Serve:

Collins Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

None.
