Gin
Type: Liquor
ABV: 37.5-50% (75-100 Proof)

Gin is a clear spirit renowned for its distinctive flavor profile primarily derived from juniper berries, which impart a pine-like and herbal taste. It is crafted through a process of distillation that includes juniper as a dominant botanical, alongside a variety of other botanicals such as coriander, citrus peel, angelica root, and sometimes spices like cinnamon or nutmeg.
This diversity in botanicals gives gin its complexity and versatility, making it suitable for a wide range of cocktails. From classic martinis to refreshing gin and tonics, gin's flavor spectrum can vary widely depending on the brand and style, from dry and juniper-forward London Dry gins to floral and citrus-forward contemporary gins.
Its origins trace back to medicinal uses in medieval Europe, evolving over centuries into a beloved and iconic spirit enjoyed worldwide for its unique botanical character and mixability in cocktails.
