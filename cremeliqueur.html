Crème Liqueur
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 15-20% (30-40 Proof)

Crème liqueur is a luxurious and indulgent type of liqueur that combines dairy cream with spirits like whiskey, rum, or vodka, along with sweeteners and various flavorings. Known for its smooth and creamy texture, it offers a rich and decadent taste experience often enhanced with notes of chocolate, vanilla, or other complementary flavors.
Crème liqueurs are typically enjoyed chilled over ice or as a dessert accompaniment, providing a luscious and satisfying treat on their own or when used creatively in culinary applications. Their creamy consistency and sweet profile make them a popular choice among those seeking a comforting and dessert-like drink.
Crème Liqueur is used in:
  • The Alexander
  • The Aviation
  • The Bramble
  • The Grasshopper
  • The Kir
  • The Russian Spring Punch
  • The Stinger
