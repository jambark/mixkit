The Vieux Carré

Ingredients:

30 ml or 1 ounce Cognac

30 ml or 1 ounce Sweet Vermouth

30 ml or 1 ounce Rye Whiskey

1 barspoon Bénédictine

2 dashes Peychaud's Bitters

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice. Stir, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Orange Zest, Maraschino Cherry.
