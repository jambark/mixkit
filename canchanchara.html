The Canchanchara

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces Cuban Aguardiente

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons Water

15 ml or .5 ounces Lime Juice

15 ml or .5 ounces Honey

Preparation:

Mix Honey, Water, and Lime Juice. Spread mixture on bottom and sides of glass. Add ice, then Rum. Stir and garnish.

Serve:

Old Fashioned Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Lime Wedge.
