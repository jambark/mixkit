The Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients:

90 ml or 3 ounces Orange Juice

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Tequila

15 ml or .5 ounces Grenadine Syrup

Preparation:

Pour Tequila and Orange Juice directly into glass with ice. Add Grenadine Syrup, do not stir. Garnish.

Serve:

Highball Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Orange Slice.
