Fernet Branca
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 39% (78 Proof)

Fernet Branca is a renowned Italian amaro prized for its bold and complex flavor profile. Made from a secret recipe of over 27 herbs and spices, it boasts intense herbal notes combined with a pronounced bitterness that distinguishes it from other amari.
Traditionally enjoyed as a digestif, Fernet Branca is often served chilled or with a splash of soda water to temper its robust flavors. Its distinct taste has garnered a global following among enthusiasts who appreciate its depth and character, making it a cherished ingredient in both cocktails and culinary creations where its unique botanical blend enhances the overall sensory experience.
Fernet Branca is used in:
  • The Fernandito
  • The Hanky Panky
