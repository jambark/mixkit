Amargo Bitters
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 44.7% (89.4 Proof)

Amargo Bitters, widely recognized for their intense and complex flavors, are essential in mixology and culinary arts. Originating from Trinidad and Tobago, these bitters contain a potent blend of herbs, spices, and botanical extracts that contribute distinctive notes such as cloves, cinnamon, and gentian root.
Used sparingly in cocktails, they enhance aroma and flavor profiles without overpowering the drink.
Beyond mixology, Amargo Bitters are valued in culinary applications, adding depth to sauces, marinades, and desserts. Their versatility and rich history make them a cherished ingredient both behind the bar and in the kitchen, offering a unique way to elevate flavors in various recipes and drinks.
