Aromatic Bitters
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 44.7% (89.4 Proof)

Aromatic bitters are concentrated flavor extracts crafted from a blend of botanicals, herbs, and spices, designed to enhance the complexity of cocktails and culinary creations.
Originating from traditional recipes, these bitters are prized for their ability to impart depth and balance to drinks without overwhelming their flavors.
Commonly used in dashes or drops, aromatic bitters contribute nuanced notes such as herbs like gentian and spices like cinnamon and cloves, adding a distinctive layer of aroma and taste.
Beyond mixology, they are also utilized in cooking to elevate sauces, marinades, and desserts, offering a versatile tool for enhancing flavor profiles in both drinks and food.
