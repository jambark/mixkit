The Mint Julep

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces Bourbon Whiskey

2 teaspoons Water

1 teaspoon Powdered Sugar

4 Mint Sprigs

Preparation:

Muddle Mint, Sugar, and Water in cup. Fill with crushed ice, then add Bourbon Whiskey and stir.

Serve:

Julep Stainless Steel Cup, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Mint Sprig.
