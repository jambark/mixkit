The Irish Coffee

Ingredients:

120 ml or 4 ounces Hot Coffee

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons Irish Whiskey

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons Cream

1 teaspoon Sugar

Preparation:

Pour hot coffee into glass. Add whiskey and sugar and stir. Pour cream on top.

Serve:

Irish Coffee Mug, Heated.

Garnishes:

None.
