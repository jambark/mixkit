Irish Whiskey
Type: Liquor
ABV: 40-46% (80-92 Proof)

Irish whiskey is renowned for its smooth and approachable character, distinct from other types of whiskey due to its triple distillation process, which contributes to a lighter and smoother flavor profile. Typically made from a blend of malted and unmalted barley, it undergoes aging in wooden casks for at least three years, allowing it to develop subtle yet complex flavors.
Common notes include hints of honey, fruit, and vanilla, reflecting the influence of the aging process. Irish whiskey holds a significant place in both historical and contemporary drinking cultures, enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or as a versatile base in cocktails. Its reputation for quality craftsmanship and rich heritage has made it a beloved choice among whiskey enthusiasts worldwide.
