Cream

Cream, a dairy product known for its high fat content, adds a luxurious and velvety texture to cocktails in mixology. Its rich flavor profile enhances the mouthfeel of drinks, providing a creamy consistency that contributes to a satisfying and indulgent drinking experience.
Cream is versatile in its application, whether used as a main ingredient to create creamy bases or as a topping to add a smooth finish to beverages. Its ability to balance flavors by softening sharp or acidic components makes it a valuable ingredient in crafting balanced and enjoyable cocktails, appealing to those who appreciate both richness and texture in their drinks.
Cream is used in:
  • The Alexander
  • The Golden Dream
  • The Grasshopper
  • The Irish Coffee
  • The Ramos Fizz
