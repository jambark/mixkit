Honey
Type: Mixer
Sweetener

Honey is a natural sweetener produced by honey bees from the nectar of flowers. It is known for its rich flavor profile, which can vary depending on the floral source, ranging from mild and floral to robust and earthy.
In mixology, honey is valued for its ability to add depth and complexity to cocktails. Bartenders often use honey as a sweetening agent, where its unique flavor enhances the overall taste profile without overpowering other ingredients.
Its versatility makes honey a popular choice for creating both classic and innovative cocktails, adding a natural sweetness and nuanced flavor that appeals to a wide range of palates.
