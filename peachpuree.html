Peach Purée
Type: Mixer
Juice

Peach puree is a smooth and thick mixture made from ripe peaches that have been peeled, pitted, and blended until smooth. It captures the sweet and juicy essence of fresh peaches, offering a vibrant flavor and rich texture.
In mixology, peach puree serves as a versatile ingredient that adds both sweetness and fruity depth to cocktails. Bartenders use peach puree to create a variety of drinks, from refreshing summer cocktails to more complex mixed drinks where its natural sweetness enhances the overall flavor profile. Its smooth consistency allows it to blend easily with other ingredients, contributing to a smooth and satisfying drinking experience.
Peach puree is particularly popular in tropical and fruity cocktails, where its bright flavor evokes the taste of ripe summer fruits and adds a delightful twist to mixed drinks.
