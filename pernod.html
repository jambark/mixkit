Pernod
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 40% (80 Proof)

Pernod is a distinguished French liqueur with a rich history dating back to the early 19th century. It was originally created by Henri-Louis Pernod in 1805 as a substitute for absinthe, a popular anise-flavored spirit that faced prohibition in the early 20th century.
Pernod's recipe blends star anise, fennel, and various aromatic herbs and spices, resulting in a complex and herbal flavor profile that sets it apart. Its production in Pontarlier, France, a renowned center for absinthe and liqueur production, ensured its reputation for quality and craftsmanship.
Over the years, Pernod has become a symbol of French culinary and drinking culture, appreciated for its versatility and ability to be enjoyed neat, with water, or as a key ingredient in enhancing the flavors of various dishes and drinks.
