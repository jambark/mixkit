The Naked and Famous

Ingredients:

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Mezcal

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Yellow Chartreuse

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Aperol

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Lime Juice

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker. Shake and strain.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

None.
