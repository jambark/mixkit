Orange Juice
Type: Mixer
Juice

Orange juice, made from freshly squeezed oranges, is renowned for its sweet and tangy flavor profile.
In mixology, orange juice serves as a versatile ingredient that adds brightness and citrusy freshness to cocktails. Its natural sweetness and acidity balance the flavors of spirits and other ingredients, contributing to a refreshing and well-rounded drinking experience.
Bartenders use orange juice in a variety of cocktails, from classic favorites to more complex creations where its vibrant flavor enhances the overall taste profile.
Whether used as a primary mixer or as a complement to other fruit juices, orange juice remains a popular choice in mixology for its ability to add both flavor and zest to cocktails, appealing to a wide range of tastes and preferences.
