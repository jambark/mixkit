Sugar Cane Juice
Type: Mixer
Sweetener

Sugar cane juice is a natural beverage extracted from pressing sugar cane stalks, known for its refreshing sweetness and distinctive grassy undertones. It is produced by crushing the stalks to release the liquid, which is then strained to remove fibers and sediment, resulting in a clear, slightly opaque juice.
Sugar cane juice is revered for its pure, unprocessed flavor that captures the essence of the tropical plant. Rich in natural sugars like sucrose, it offers a refreshing alternative to refined sugar in beverages and culinary creations.
Commonly enjoyed fresh and chilled, sugar cane juice is a popular street food and ingredient in various cultures around the world, prized for its ability to impart a unique sweetness that complements both sweet and savory dishes. Its natural freshness and flavor make it a delightful addition to mixology, where it can be used to craft inventive cocktails and mocktails that highlight its tropical essence.
