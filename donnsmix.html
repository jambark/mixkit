Donn's Mix
Type: Mixer
Sauce

Donn's Mix is a specialized ingredient in mixology, created by combining grapefruit juice and cinnamon syrup. Named after Donn Beach, a pioneering figure in the tiki bar scene, Donn's Mix offers a unique flavor profile that blends the tangy citrus notes of grapefruit with the warm, spicy sweetness of cinnamon.
This combination creates a versatile mixer that adds depth and complexity to cocktails without overpowering other flavors. Bartenders use Donn's Mix to enhance tropical and exotic drinks, where its distinctive taste adds a nuanced layer of flavor and aroma. Its popularity in tiki-style cocktails underscores its role in creating balanced and flavorful drinks that evoke the spirit of the islands.
