Cointreau
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 40% (80 Proof)

Cointreau is a renowned French orange liqueur celebrated for its refined and intense citrus flavor, crafted from a blend of sweet and bitter orange peels. Established in 1849, Cointreau distinguishes itself from Triple Sec through its meticulous triple-distillation process using sugar beet alcohol, which contributes to its smooth and sophisticated profile.
Unlike many Triple Secs, which vary widely in quality and sweetness, Cointreau maintains a consistent balance that enhances both the complexity of mixed drinks and the vibrant essence it lends to culinary creations.
Its versatility and premium reputation make it a favored choice among mixologists and chefs alike for its ability to impart a bright and aromatic orange character to a variety of dishes and libations.
Cointreau is used in:
  • The Corpse Reviver #2
  • The Cosmopolitan
  • The Long Island Iced Tea
  • The Singapore Sling
