Peach Schnapps
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 15-20% (30-40 Proof)

Peach Schnapps is a beloved fruit liqueur celebrated for its sweet and distinctly peachy flavor. Originating from Germany, Schnapps historically referred to any strong distilled spirit. Over time, Peach Schnapps evolved into a liqueur made by infusing peach extracts with a base spirit like vodka or grain alcohol, resulting in its signature fruity taste.
Its popularity soared in the 1970s and 1980s, becoming a staple in bars and households alike for its versatility in adding a luscious peach flavor to a wide array of drinks and culinary creations. Beyond its use in beverages, Peach Schnapps has found its way into desserts and sauces, enhancing dishes with its vibrant fruitiness and contributing to its enduring appeal in both casual and sophisticated settings.
