Cachaça
Type: Liquor
ABV: 38-48% (76-96 Proof)

Cachaça is a Brazilian distilled spirit crafted from fermented sugarcane juice, distinguishing itself as one of Brazil's most iconic beverages. Produced through fermentation and distillation, cachaça can be categorized into industrial and artisanal varieties, each offering distinct flavor profiles ranging from vegetal and slightly grassy to more complex notes depending on production methods and aging.
It is widely enjoyed both domestically and internationally, prominently featured in the quintessential Brazilian cocktail, the Caipirinha, alongside other mixed drinks. Cachaça holds cultural significance in Brazil, protected by geographical indication laws, and continues to be cherished for its versatile use in cocktails and its role in Brazilian culinary traditions.
