The Barracuda

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces Pineapple Juice

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Gold Rum

15 ml or .5 ounces Galliano

1 dash Lime Juice

Prosecco, as needed

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients except Prosecco into cocktail shaker with ice. Shake and strain. Top glass with Prosecco.

Serve:

Highball Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Pineapple Slice, Maraschino Cherry, Mint Sprig.
