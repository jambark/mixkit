Orange Bitters
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 28-40% (56-80 Proof)

Orange bitters are essential cocktail ingredients crafted from the essence of bitter and sweet orange peels, along with a blend of spices and botanicals. Known for their vibrant citrusy flavor profile, they add a nuanced layer of complexity to cocktails without dominating the overall taste.
Orange bitters are used sparingly to enhance the aromatic qualities and balance of drinks, particularly in classic cocktails like the Martini and Old Fashioned.
Beyond mixology, they are also employed in culinary applications to subtly elevate flavors in sauces, marinades, and desserts, showcasing their versatility in enhancing both cocktails and cuisine with their distinctive citrus and herbal notes.
Orange Bitters are used in:
  • The Casino
  • The Martinez
  • The Tuxedo
