The Gin Fizz

Ingredients:

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Gin

30 ml or 1 ounce Lemon Juice

10 ml or 2 teaspoons Simple Syrup

Splash of Soda Water

Preparation:

Add all ingredients except soda water into mixing glass with ice cubes. Stir and strain, then top with soda water and garnish.

Serve:

Old Fashioned Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Lemon Slice.
