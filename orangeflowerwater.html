Orange Flower Water
Type: Mixer
Herbal

Orange flower water, derived from the distillation of bitter orange blossoms, is a fragrant and aromatic ingredient used in mixology to impart a delicate floral essence to cocktails. It adds a subtle and sophisticated floral note with hints of citrus that enhance the overall aroma and flavor profile of drinks.
Bartenders utilize orange flower water sparingly due to its potent nature, often adding just a few drops to cocktails to infuse them with a floral complexity without overwhelming other ingredients. Its use harkens back to classic cocktail recipes where it was employed to elevate the fragrance and taste, particularly in drinks with Mediterranean or Middle Eastern influences
Orange flower water remains a cherished ingredient in mixology for its ability to provide a nuanced and aromatic layer that enhances the drinking experience.
