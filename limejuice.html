Lime Juice
Type: Mixer
Juice

Lime juice, extracted from the citrus fruit lime, is renowned in mixology for its crisp and tangy flavor profile. It offers a refreshing burst of citrus acidity that brightens and balances cocktails, adding depth and complexity to their flavor profiles.
Bartenders use freshly squeezed lime juice to provide a zesty and vibrant element that ranges from sharp and tart to subtly sweet, depending on the lime's ripeness. Lime juice's acidity not only enhances the taste of cocktails but also serves as a natural preservative, ensuring drinks maintain their freshness.
It is a fundamental ingredient in numerous classic and contemporary cocktails, where its versatility complements a wide range of spirits and mixers, contributing to a crisp and satisfying drinking experience.
