Ginger Ale
Type: Mixer
Beverage

Ginger Ale is a carbonated soft drink flavored with ginger, known for its refreshing and slightly spicy taste.
In mixology, ginger ale serves as a versatile mixer that adds a crisp effervescence and a mild ginger flavor to cocktails. Its sweetness and subtle spice make it a popular choice for balancing stronger spirits or enhancing the aromatic complexity of drinks.
Bartenders often use ginger ale to create refreshing highball cocktails or to mellow out the sharpness of spirits in various mixed drinks. Its bubbly texture and gentle ginger heat contribute to a pleasant drinking experience, appealing to a wide range of palates in both classic and modern cocktail recipes.
