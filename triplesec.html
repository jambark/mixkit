Triple Sec
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 15-40% (30-80 Proof)

Triple Sec is a popular orange-flavored liqueur recognized for its versatility and sweet citrus essence. Unlike Cointreau, which is known for its refined and intense orange flavor derived from a blend of sweet and bitter orange peels, Triple Sec varies widely in quality and sweetness levels depending on the brand and production method.
Triple Sec is typically made by distilling orange peels or using orange flavoring with a base spirit such as neutral grain alcohol. Its taste profile can range from moderately sweet to very sweet, making it suitable for a broad range of mixed drinks and culinary recipes where its bright orange flavor adds depth and sweetness.
While Cointreau is often prized for its premium quality and balanced citrus profile, Triple Sec offers a more accessible and diverse range of options depending on personal taste preferences and intended use in both drinks and food.
Triple Sec is used in:
  • The Between the Sheets
  • The Golden Dream
  • The Lemon Drop
  • The Margarita
  • The Sidecar
