Curaçao
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 15-40% (30-80 Proof)

Curaçao is a versatile liqueur crafted from the dried peels of bitter oranges, renowned for its vibrant citrus flavor and distinctively colored varieties such as blue, orange, and clear.
Originating from the Caribbean island of Curaçao, this liqueur is characterized by its sweet and slightly bitter taste, achieved through the infusion of orange peels and often enhanced with spices or herbs.
Curaçao adds depth and complexity to cocktails and mixed drinks, lending both citrusy brightness and a touch of sweetness to various recipes. Beyond mixology, it is also used in culinary applications to impart a tangy orange flavor to desserts, sauces, and marinades, showcasing its versatility in both the bar and kitchen.
Curaçao is used in:
  • The Brandy Crusta
  • The Mai Tai
Orange Curaçao
In The Mai Tai, the Curaçao which is used is specified as Orange Curaçao.
