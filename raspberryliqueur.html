Raspberry Liqueur
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 15-25% (30-50 Proof)

Raspberry liqueur is a delightful and versatile spirit infused with the essence of ripe raspberries, known for its vibrant red color and sweet, fruity flavor profile. Produced by macerating raspberries in a base spirit such as vodka or brandy, it captures the essence of the fruit, balancing its natural sweetness with a hint of tartness.
This liqueur adds a luscious raspberry flavor to a variety of culinary applications, including desserts, sauces, and even savory dishes, enhancing their taste with a burst of fruity richness.
Its bright and aromatic qualities make it a popular choice for those seeking to incorporate a touch of berry essence into both drinks and food recipes, offering a refreshing and flavorful addition to any culinary adventure.
