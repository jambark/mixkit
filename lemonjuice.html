Lemon Juice
Type: Mixer
Juice

Lemon juice, derived from the citrus fruit lemon, is prized in mixology for its bright, tangy flavor and acidic bite. It adds a refreshing and zesty element to cocktails, balancing sweetness and enhancing the overall complexity of drinks.
Bartenders use freshly squeezed lemon juice to provide a burst of citrus flavor that can range from tart to subtly sweet, depending on the ripeness of the fruit. Its acidity also acts as a natural preservative, ensuring cocktails maintain their freshness and vibrancy. Lemon juice is a staple in both classic and modern cocktails, where its versatile flavor profile pairs well with a variety of spirits and mixers, contributing to a refreshing and enjoyable drinking experience.
