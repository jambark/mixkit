The Horse's Neck

Ingredients:

120 ml or 4 ounces Ginger Ale

40 ml or 1 ounce and 2 teaspoons Brandy

1 dash of Angostura Bitters

Preparation:

Pour Brandy and Ginger Ale directly into glass with ice. Stir and add bitters, then garnish.

Serve:

Highball Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Lemon Zest.
