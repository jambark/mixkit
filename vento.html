The Ve.n.to

Ingredients:

45 ml White Smooth Grappa

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Lemon Juice

15 ml or .5 ounces Honey Mix

15 ml or .5 ounces Chamomile Cordial

10 ml or 2 teaspoons Egg White

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker. Shake, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Tumbler, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Lemon Zest, White Grapes.
