Salt
Type: Mixer
Seasoning

Salt is a fundamental ingredient renowned for its ability to enhance flavors and balance taste profiles across culinary realms.
Derived from seawater or mined from deposits, salt is primarily composed of sodium chloride. Its crystalline structure and saline taste lend a distinctive character to dishes, from savory to sweet.
In mixology, salt plays a crucial role in tempering bitterness and accentuating sweetness, showcasing its versatility beyond traditional culinary applications. Bartenders and chefs alike utilize salt to craft nuanced cocktails and dishes, where a pinch can elevate flavors, amplify aromas, and provide a harmonious contrast that complements a wide range of ingredients.
