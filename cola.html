Cola
Type: Mixer
Beverage

Cola is a carbonated soft drink flavored with a combination of vanilla, cinnamon, citrus oils, and other spices. It is widely recognized for its sweet and slightly acidic taste, which comes from ingredients such as caramelized sugar or corn syrup.
In mixology, cola serves as a versatile mixer that adds sweetness, effervescence, and a distinct flavor profile to cocktails. It pairs well with spirits like rum and whiskey, offering a familiar and refreshing base for classic cocktails such as rum and cola or whiskey cola.
Cola's unique flavor and carbonation also make it suitable for creating more complex mixed drinks, blending with various liqueurs, juices, or bitters to create balanced and flavorful cocktails that appeal to a wide range of tastes. Its popularity and availability ensure that cola remains a staple in both classic and contemporary mixology.
Cola is used in:
  • The Cuba Libre
  • The Fernandito
  • The Long Island Iced Tea
