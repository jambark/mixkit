Elderflower Cordial
Type: Mixer
Herbal

Elderflower cordial is a sweet, floral syrup made from elderflower blossoms, sugar, and water. It captures the delicate and aromatic essence of elderflowers, offering a subtly sweet flavor with floral undertones.
In mixology, elderflower cordial is prized for its unique floral profile, which adds a sophisticated and fragrant touch to cocktails. Bartenders use it to impart a gentle floral note that enhances the overall aroma and complexity of drinks without overpowering other ingredients.
Elderflower cordial is versatile and pairs well with a variety of spirits and mixers, contributing a refreshing and aromatic quality to both classic and modern cocktail recipes. Its popularity in mixology reflects a growing appreciation for botanical flavors and the desire to create nuanced and elegant drinking experiences.
