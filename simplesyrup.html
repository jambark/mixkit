Simple Syrup
Type: Mixer
Sweetener

Simple syrup, a staple in the mixologist's toolkit, is a straightforward yet essential ingredient made from equal parts sugar and water. Its clear, viscous consistency and neutral flavor make it a versatile sweetener that effortlessly blends into beverages without overpowering other ingredients.
By dissolving sugar in water over low heat and then cooling the mixture, simple syrup achieves a balanced sweetness that enhances cocktails and mocktails alike.
Its purpose extends beyond sweetness; it also adds texture and mouthfeel, contributing to a smooth and well-rounded drinking experience. Bartenders value simple syrup for its ability to evenly distribute sweetness throughout a drink, ensuring each sip is perfectly balanced and delightful.
