The Clover Club

Ingredients:

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Gin

15 ml or .5 ounces Lemon Juice

15 ml or .5 ounces Raspberry Syrup

1 Egg White -or- 20 ml or 4 teaspoons Aquafaba -or- 4 drops Cocktail Foamer

Preparation:

Dry shake ingredients in cocktail strainer to emulsify, add ice, shake and strain.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

3 Raspberries.
