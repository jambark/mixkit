The Sex on the Beach

Ingredients:

40 ml or 1 ounce and 2 teaspoons Vodka

40 ml or 1 ounce and 2 teaspoons Orange Juice

40 ml or 1 ounce and 2 teaspoons Cranberry Juice

20 ml or 4 teaspoons Peach Schnapps

Preparation:

Build all ingredients directly into glass with ice, then garnish.

Serve:

Highball Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Orange Slice.
