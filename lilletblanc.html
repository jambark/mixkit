Lillet Blanc
Type: Liquor
ABV: 17% (34 Proof)

Lillet Blanc is a classic French aperitif wine originating from Bordeaux, crafted from a blend of Bordeaux wines and citrus liqueurs. Created in 1872 by the Lillet brothers, it boasts a golden hue and a fragrant bouquet of honey, orange, and floral notes.
Lillet Blanc offers a balanced and refreshing taste on its own, served chilled, or over ice. Its versatility extends to being a favored ingredient in cocktails, including the iconic Vesper Martini.
Renowned for its elegance and cultural resonance, Lillet Blanc continues to captivate aficionados as a symbol of sophisticated enjoyment before a meal or as a delightful complement to social gatherings.
Lillet Blanc is used in:
  • The Corpse Reviver #2
  • The Vesper
