The Martinez

Ingredients:

45 ml or 1.5 ounces London Dry Gin

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Sweet Red Vermouth

1 barspoon Maraschino Liqueur

2 dashes Orange Bitters

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into a mixing glass. Stir, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Lemon Zest.
