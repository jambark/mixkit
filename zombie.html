The Zombie

Ingredients:

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Jamaican Dark Rum

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Puerto Rican Gold Rum

30 ml or 1 ounce Demerara Rum

20 ml or 4 teaspoons Lime Juice

15 ml or .5 ounces Falernum

15 ml or .5 ounces Donn's Mix

1 teaspoon Grenadine Syrup

1 dash Angostura Bitters

6 drops Pernod

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients in cocktail shaker with crushed ice. Shake, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Zombie Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Mint Sprig.
