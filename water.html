Water
Type: Mixer
Beverage

Water is a fundamental substance that's clear, colorless, and tasteless. It's essential for life, hydration, and everyday cooking. In mixology, water is used to dilute drinks, adjust textures, and balance flavors, ensuring beverages taste just right. Its simplicity and versatility make water indispensable in both daily life and the kitchen.
Water is used in:
  • The Canchanchara
  • The Mint Julep
  • The Old Fashioned
