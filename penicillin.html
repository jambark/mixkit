The Penicillin

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces blended Scotch Whiskey

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Lemon Juice

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Honey Syrup

7.5 ml or 1.5 teaspoons Lagavulin 16y Whiskey

2-3 quarter sized slices of Ginger

Preparation:

Muddle Ginger in cocktail shaker, then add all remaining ingredents except Bourbon Whiskey. Fill with ice, shake, and double-strain. Then top with Bourbon Whiskey and garnish.

Serve:

Old Fashioned Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Candied Ginger.
