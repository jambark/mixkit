Galliano
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 30% (60 Proof)

Galliano is a vibrant Italian herbal liqueur renowned for its striking yellow color and complex flavor profile. Infused with a blend of herbs, spices, vanilla, and anise, it offers a distinctive taste experience that balances sweetness with herbal and aromatic notes.
Galliano's origins date back to the late 19th century, and its unique bottle shape has become an iconic symbol in the world of spirits. Traditionally enjoyed as a digestif, Galliano's versatility extends to culinary uses where it enhances desserts and sauces, showcasing its depth and appeal beyond the bar.
Galliano is used in:
  • The Barracuda
  • The Golden Dream
  • The Yellow Bird
