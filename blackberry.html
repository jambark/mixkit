Blackberry
Type: Garnish
Fruit

Blackberries are cherished in mixology for their robust and slightly tart flavor profile, characterized by a balance of sweetness and acidity. Their deep purple hue and juicy texture make them a versatile ingredient in crafting cocktails.
When used in mixology, blackberries are often muddled or pureed to extract their vibrant juices and essence, which infuse drinks with a rich, fruity complexity. Their natural sweetness pairs well with a wide range of spirits, from clear options like vodka and gin to aged spirits such as whiskey or brandy, adding depth and enhancing the overall flavor profile of cocktails.
Whether used as a primary flavor component or as a subtle accent, blackberries contribute a refreshing and aromatic element to drinks, appealing to both casual enthusiasts and seasoned mixologists seeking to elevate their creations with a touch of berry freshness.
Blackberry is used in:
  • The Bramble
  • The Russian Spring Punch
