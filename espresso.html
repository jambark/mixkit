Espresso
Type: Mixer
Aromatic

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure. This method extracts the essence of the beans, resulting in a small, potent shot known for its bold flavor and intense aroma.
In mixology, espresso is valued for its versatility as an ingredient that adds depth and complexity to drinks. Its robust profile, characterized by dark chocolate notes and a rich, bitter-sweet taste, enhances the overall flavor profile of cocktails.
Bartenders often utilize espresso to provide a deep, roasted undertone that complements various spirits and mixers, contributing to a sophisticated and flavorful drinking experience. Beyond its taste, espresso's caffeine content can also lend a stimulating element to cocktails, making it a sought-after ingredient for creating both classic and innovative mixed drinks.
