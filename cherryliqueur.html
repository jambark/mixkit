Cherry Liqueur
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 20-30% (40-60 Proof)

Cherry Liqueur is a delightful and versatile liqueur infused with the essence of cherries or cherry flavorings, often sweetened with sugar and sometimes complemented by hints of almond or other botanicals.
Known for its rich and fruity profile, cherry liqueur offers a distinct taste of ripe cherries that can range from sweet to slightly tart depending on the specific blend and production method. It is enjoyed as a standalone digestif, served over ice, and also finds its place as a key ingredient in a variety of cocktails and culinary creations.
Whether enhancing the flavor of desserts, adding depth to sauces, or lending a fruity twist to cocktails, cherry liqueur is cherished for its ability to impart a vibrant and enticing cherry flavor to a wide range of drinks and dishes.

  • The Singapore Sling
