Celery Salt
Type: Mixer
Seasoning

Celery salt, a dry seasoning blend composed of ground celery seeds and salt, contributes a unique savory flavor profile to mixology. Its taste is distinctly celery-like but more concentrated and salty, with a hint of bitterness.
In mixology, celery salt is employed primarily as a seasoning to enhance the savory character of cocktails, particularly those with vegetable or herbal components. It can be used to rim glasses for drinks like Bloody Marys or incorporated directly into cocktail recipes to add depth and complexity.
Celery salt's ability to impart a robust savory note makes it a versatile ingredient, often chosen to complement flavors such as tomato juice, vodka, and other savory ingredients commonly found in cocktails.
