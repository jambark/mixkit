Maraschino Liqueur
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 30-32% (60-64 Proof)

Maraschino liqueur, exemplified by Maraschino Luxardo, is a renowned Italian spirit crafted from Marasca cherries and their pits, which impart a distinctive almond-like flavor with subtle hints of cherry. This clear liqueur is known for its complex and slightly sweet taste, making it a versatile ingredient in both mixology and culinary applications.
Maraschino Luxardo, produced by the Luxardo family since 1821, stands out for its rich history and traditional production methods, ensuring a premium quality product. Beyond cocktails, Maraschino liqueur enhances the flavor of desserts, sauces, and even savory dishes, showcasing its broad culinary appeal and enduring popularity in both traditional and modern recipes.
