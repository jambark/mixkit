Vodka
Type: Liquor
ABV: 35-50% (70-100 Proof)

Vodka is a clear, neutral-flavored distilled spirit that originated in Eastern Europe and Russia. Traditionally made from grains like wheat or rye, though sometimes from potatoes or fruits in modern variations, vodka is known for its versatility. Its mild taste allows it to blend seamlessly into a wide range of cocktails and mixed drinks, making it a popular choice both in classic concoctions such as the Martini and the Moscow Mule, and in contemporary creations.
Vodka's clean profile and ability to harmonize with other flavors have made it a staple in bars and households worldwide, prized for its mixability and broad appeal across different drinking cultures.
Vodka is used in:
  • The Black Russian
  • The Bloody Mary
  • The Cosmopolitan
  • The Espresso Martini
  • The French Martini
  • The Lemon Drop
  • The Long Island Iced Tea
  • The Moscow Mule
  • The Russian Spring Punch
  • The Sea Breeze
  • The Sex on the Beach
  • The Spicy Fifty
  • The Vesper
