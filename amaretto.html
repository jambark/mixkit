Amaretto
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 28-32% (56-64 Proof)

Amaretto is a sweet, almond-flavored liqueur that originates from Italy. Its distinct flavor profile comes from bitter almonds or apricot kernels, which are infused or distilled with a base spirit, sugar, and sometimes herbs and spices.
The liqueur is characterized by its rich, nutty taste with notes of marzipan and vanilla, often accompanied by a hint of caramel or fruitiness. Amaretto is versatile and commonly enjoyed on its own as a digestif, in cocktails, or used in desserts such as Tiramisu or drizzled over ice cream. Its popularity has spread globally, becoming a staple in both bars and home liquor cabinets.
