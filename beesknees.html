The Bee's Knees

Ingredients:

52.5 ml or 1.75 ounces Dry Gin

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Lemon Juice

22.5 ml or 1.5 tablespoons Orange Juice

2 teaspoons Honey Syrup

Preparation:

Pour Lemon Juice and Orange Juice into cocktail shaker. Add Honey Syrup and stir. Add Gin and ice, shake, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Lemon Zest.
