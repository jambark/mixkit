Orgeat Syrup
Type: Mixer
Sweetener

Orgeat syrup is a sweet and almond-flavored syrup made from almonds, sugar, and orange flower water. It is known for its delicate almond taste with floral and citrus undertones, lending a unique complexity to cocktails.
In mixology, orgeat syrup is prized for its versatility as a sweetening agent that also adds a subtle nutty and aromatic profile to drinks. Bartenders use orgeat syrup to enhance both classic and modern cocktails, where its flavor complements a variety of spirits and mixers. It is particularly prominent in tiki-style cocktails, such as the Mai Tai, where its almond essence pairs harmoniously with rum and citrus flavors to create a balanced and flavorful drink.
Orgeat syrup's ability to contribute sweetness and depth without overpowering other ingredients makes it a valued component in mixology for crafting nuanced and enjoyable cocktails.
Orgeat Syrup is used in:
  • The Mai Tai
  • The Trinidad Sour
