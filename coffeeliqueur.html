Coffee Liqueur
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 20-25% (40-50 Proof)

Coffee liqueur is celebrated for its rich and robust coffee flavor, achieved through infusing or blending coffee beans or extracts with spirits like vodka or rum. This liqueur often includes sweeteners and other flavorings to enhance its depth and complexity, offering a smooth and indulgent taste reminiscent of freshly brewed coffee.
Coffee liqueur is highly versatile, enjoyed in a variety of ways from enhancing desserts to complementing coffee-based beverages. Its distinctive coffee profile makes it a popular choice for both mixing into drinks and savoring on its own, providing a delightful alternative for coffee enthusiasts seeking a spirited twist.
Coffee Liqueur is used in:
  • The Black Russian
  • The Espresso Martini
Kahlúa
In The Espresso Martini, the Coffee Liqueur which is used is specified as Kahlúa.
