The Americano

Ingredients:

30 ml or 1 ounce Campari

30 ml or 1 ounce Sweet Red Vermouth

A splash of Soda Water

Preparation:

Add ice into an old fashioned glass, pour over with Campari and vermouth, add a splash of soda water and garnish with half an orange slice and a lemon twist.

Serve:

Old Fashioned Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Half Orange Slice, Lemon Twist.
