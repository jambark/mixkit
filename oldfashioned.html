The Old Fashioned

Ingredients:

45 ml or 1.5 ounces Whiskey

1 Sugar Cube

A few dashes Angostura Bitters

A few dashes Plain Water

Preparation:

In the glass: Place sugar cube, saturate with bitters, add water, and muddle until dissolved. Fill with ice cubes and add whiskey. Stir and garnish.

Serve:

Old fashioned Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Orange Slice, Maraschino Cherry.
