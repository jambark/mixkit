The Tipperary

Ingredients:

50 ml or 1 ounce and 4 teaspoons Irish Whiskey

25 ml or 5 teaspoons Sweet Red Vermouth

15 ml or .5 ounces Chartreuse

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice. Stir, strain, and garnish.

Serve:

Cocktail Glass, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Orange Slice.
