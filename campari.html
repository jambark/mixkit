Campari
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 20.5-28.5% (41-57 Proof)

Campari is a renowned Italian spirit celebrated for its striking red hue and complex flavor profile. Crafted from a proprietary blend of herbs, spices, and fruits, its distinctive taste combines bitter and sweet notes in a harmonious balance. This iconic liqueur is a staple ingredient in numerous cocktails, including the classic Negroni and Americano, where it adds depth and character.
Beyond mixology, Campari's bold flavor profile makes it a versatile ingredient in culinary applications, enhancing sauces, marinades, and even desserts with its unique blend of botanicals and aromatic complexity. Its rich history and vibrant presence have cemented Campari's status as a beloved icon in the world of spirits and flavors.
Campari is used in:
  • The Americano
  • The Boulevardier
  • The Negroni
Bitter Campari
In The Boulevardier, the Campari which is used is specified as Bitter Campari.
