Grapefruit Juice
Type: Mixer
Juice

Grapefruit Juice is a tangy and citrusy beverage made from the juice of grapefruit, known for its refreshing flavor and bright acidity.
In mixology, grapefruit juice serves as a versatile ingredient that adds a crisp and tart profile to cocktails. Its distinctive flavor enhances both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, providing a balance of sweetness and acidity that complements a variety of spirits.
Bartenders often use grapefruit juice to create refreshing highball cocktails or to add a citrusy twist to classic mixed drinks. Its vibrant taste and refreshing qualities make grapefruit juice a popular choice in mixology for crafting cocktails that are both invigorating and satisfyingly tart.
Grapefruit Juice is used in:
  • The Hemingway Special
  • The Sea Breeze
