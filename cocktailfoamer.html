Cocktail Foamer
Type: Mixer
Emulsifier

A cocktail foamer is an additive used in mixology to create a frothy and creamy texture in cocktails, enhancing both visual appeal and mouthfeel. It typically consists of ingredients like foaming agents, stabilizers, and sometimes emulsifiers designed to produce a stable foam when shaken with other cocktail components.
Cocktail foamers are especially useful for adding a luxurious and velvety texture to drinks without altering their flavor profile significantly. They are popular in modern mixology for their ability to elevate the drinking experience by providing a silky smooth texture that complements various cocktail styles, from sours to fizzes, enhancing both the aesthetic presentation and overall enjoyment of the drink.
