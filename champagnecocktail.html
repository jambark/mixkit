The Champagne Cocktail

Ingredients:

90 ml or 3 ounces Champagne

10 ml or 2 teaspoons Cognac

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

1 Sugar Cube

Preparation:

Add dash of Angostura Bitter onto the sugar cube and drop into flute. Pour Cognac, then Champagne. Garnish.

Serve:

Champagne Flute, Chilled.

Garnishes:

Orange Slice, Maraschino Cherry.
