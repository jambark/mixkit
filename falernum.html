Falernum
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 11-20% (22-40 Proof)

Falernum is a Caribbean syrupy liqueur celebrated for its distinctive blend of flavors, including lime, almond, ginger, and sometimes cloves. This versatile ingredient adds a complex layer of sweetness, spice, and citrusy brightness to beverages.
Originally from Barbados, Falernum is prized for its ability to enhance tropical drinks and cocktails, offering a harmonious balance of flavors that complements rum-based concoctions particularly well.
Beyond mixology, Falernum's aromatic profile and subtle sweetness make it a popular choice in culinary applications, where it elevates the taste of desserts, sauces, and even marinades, showcasing its versatility and exotic appeal.
