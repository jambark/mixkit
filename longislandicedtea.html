The Long Island Iced Tea

Ingredients:

30 ml or 1 ounce Lemon Juice

20 ml or 4 teaspoons Simple Syrup

15 ml or .5 ounces Tequila

15 ml or .5 ounces Vodka

15 ml or .5 ounces White Rum

15 ml or .5 ounces Cointreau

15 ml or .5 ounces Gin

Top with Cola

Preparation:

Pour ingredients directly into glass. Stir and garnish.

Serve:

Highball Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Lemon Slice.
