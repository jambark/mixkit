Chamomile Cordial
Type: Mixer
Herbal

Chamomile cordial is a sweet, floral syrup made by infusing chamomile flowers with sugar and water. It captures the delicate aroma and soothing qualities of chamomile, offering a subtle honey-like sweetness with floral undertones.
In mixology, chamomile cordial is valued for its unique flavor profile, which adds a gentle floral note to cocktails without overpowering other ingredients. Bartenders often use it to impart a light, aromatic complexity to drinks, enhancing their depth and providing a soothing background flavor.
Chamomile cordial pairs well with a variety of spirits and mixers, offering versatility in crafting both refreshing and more complex cocktails. Its use reflects a growing trend in mixology towards incorporating herbal and botanical elements to create nuanced and aromatic drinking experiences.
