Aquafaba
Type: Mixer
Emulsifier

Aquafaba is the viscous liquid leftover from cooking legumes, most commonly chickpeas. It gained popularity as a vegan egg substitute due to its unique ability to mimic egg whites when whipped. In mixology, aquafaba serves as a versatile ingredient for creating vegan-friendly foams in cocktails.
When shaken vigorously with other cocktail ingredients, aquafaba emulsifies and aerates, producing a frothy texture similar to that achieved with egg whites. This foam adds a creamy mouthfeel and enhances the overall presentation of cocktails without altering their flavor profile.
Bartenders appreciate aquafaba for its neutral taste and ability to bind ingredients, making it suitable for a variety of cocktail styles, from sours to fizzes. Its use also caters to patrons with dietary restrictions or preferences, offering a plant-based alternative to traditional cocktail ingredients.
