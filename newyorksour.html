The New York Sour

Ingredients:

60 ml or 2 ounces Whiskey

30 ml or .5 ounces Lemon Juice

22.5 or 1.5 tablespoons Simple Syrup

15 ml Red Wine

Few drops Egg White

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients except Red Wine into cocktail shaker. Shake and strain. Pour Red Wine and garnish.

Serve:

Rocks Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Lemon Zest, Maraschino Cherry.
