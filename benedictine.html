Bénédictine
Type: Liqueur
ABV: 40% (80 Proof)

Bénédictine is a renowned herbal liqueur originating from France in the 19th century, crafted from a secret blend of 27 different plants and spices. Its complex flavor profile combines herbal, spicy, and slightly sweet notes, creating a distinctive taste that is both aromatic and rich.
Originally created by monks, Bénédictine is celebrated for its versatility, adding depth and complexity to various culinary and beverage experiences. Beyond its use in mixology, it is also cherished as a digestif enjoyed neat or on the rocks, showcasing its centuries-old tradition of craftsmanship and flavor mastery.
Bénédictine is used in:
  • The Singapore Sling
  • The Vieux Carre
