The Suffering Bastard

Ingredients:

30 ml or 1 ounce Cognac or Brandy

30 ml or 1 ounce Gin

15 ml or .5 ounces Lime Juice

2 dashes Angostura Bitters

Ginger Beer, as needed

Preparation:

Pour all ingredients except Ginger Beer into cocktail shaker with ice. Shake and pour unstrained into glass. Top with Ginger Beer.

Serve:

Collins Glass, On the Rocks.

Garnishes:

Mint Sprigs, Orange Slice.
