Coconut Cream
Type: Mixer
Cream

Coconut cream is a rich and creamy substance derived from the flesh of mature coconuts. It has a thick consistency similar to dairy cream but is dairy-free, making it popular in vegan and dairy-free recipes. In mixology, coconut cream is used to impart a smooth and velvety texture to cocktails, adding a tropical sweetness and richness that complements various flavors.
It is commonly found in tiki-style drinks and other tropical cocktails where its coconut flavor enhances the overall exotic and creamy profile of the beverage. Coconut cream can be mixed directly into drinks or used as a garnish, providing both flavor and a pleasing creamy mouthfeel that contributes to a satisfying drinking experience. Its versatility makes it a staple ingredient in creating creamy and indulgent cocktails that evoke the flavors of the tropics.
